ArcticConnect Portal

ArcticConnect is a network-enabled platform for realizing geospatial referencing of information about the arctic system derived from research, education, and private sector activities in the arctic and subarctic. This project is maintained by the Arctic Institute of North America, the GeoSensorWeb Lab at the University of Calgary, Cybera, Inc, and CANARIE.

Our public services include Arctic Web Map and Arctic Sensor Web.
